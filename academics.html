CBSE Curriculum Overview

Affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), our curriculum blends national standards with global best practices to produce well-rounded, future-ready students.

Classes 1–5

Primary Education

Building strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and curiosity through activity-based learning.

Focus Areas:

Foundational literacy & numeracy, environmental studies, arts & crafts, music, physical education, and social-emotional learning.

Deepening knowledge across disciplines, with introduction of specialized subjects and career awareness.

Pedagogy

Our Learning Methodology

We combine time-tested teaching practices with modern pedagogical approaches for maximum student engagement and learning.

Activity-Based Learning

Students learn by doing — experiments, projects, and real-world problem solving build deep understanding.

Collaborative Learning

Group projects and peer learning develop teamwork, communication, and leadership skills.

Technology Integration

Smart boards, educational apps, and coding tools bring lessons alive and prepare students for the digital world.

Critical Thinking

Socratic discussions, debates, and case studies challenge students to think beyond rote memorization.

Differentiated Instruction

Teachers adapt content and pace to meet every learner's individual needs, ensuring no child is left behind.

Assessment

Examination System

Our assessment framework is designed to measure comprehensive learning, not just memory retention.

Assessment Structure

Periodic Tests (20 marks)

Conducted every month or 6 weeks to gauge ongoing comprehension and identify gaps early.

Half-Yearly Examination (80 marks)

Comprehensive written test covering all syllabus from April to September. Full marks breakdown provided.

Annual Examination (80 marks)

Full-year assessment in March. Combined with internal assessments for final grade computation.

Internal Assessment (20 marks)

Includes portfolio, practicals, projects, speaking skills, and class participation scores.
