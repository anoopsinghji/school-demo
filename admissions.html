Admission Process

Our simple, transparent 4-step process ensures a smooth experience for every family.

1

Submit Inquiry Form

Fill the inquiry form below or visit the school office. Our team will contact you within 24 hours.

2

Document Submission

Submit the required documents at the Admissions Office. We verify and prepare your child's file.

3

Interaction / Assessment

A friendly interaction with the child (and parent for Classes 1–5) to understand aptitude and ensure the right fit.

4

Admission Confirmation

Receive the offer letter and pay the first installment of fees to confirm your child's admission.
