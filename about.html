Our Story

38 Years of Shaping Futures

Founded in 1985 by visionary educator Dr. Ramesh Edgemont, the Academy began with just 200 students and 15 teachers in a modest building in Civil Lines, New Delhi. The vision was simple but powerful: to provide every child with an education that was rigorous, joyful, and deeply humane.

Over the decades, Edgemont School grew into one of Delhi's most respected educational institutions, earning recognition from CBSE and national education boards. Today, with over 3,200 students, 180 faculty members, and a sprawling 8-acre campus, we continue to honor that founding vision.

Our alumni include doctors, engineers, artists, entrepreneurs, civil servants, and global leaders — all united by the values they imbibed at Edgemont.

1985

Foundation

Dr. Ramesh Edgemont establishes the Academy with 200 students in Civil Lines, Delhi.

1993

CBSE Affiliation

Receives full CBSE affiliation and introduces Classes 11–12 with Science and Commerce streams.

2002

New Campus

Expansion to the current 8-acre campus with modern labs, sports complex, and auditorium.

2015

Digital Transformation

Launch of Smart Classrooms across all grades and introduction of the STEM program.

2024

National Recognition

Awarded "Best School in Delhi NCR" by Education Today magazine for the 3rd consecutive year.

Vision, Mission & Values

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized institution that nurtures compassionate, intellectually curious, and socially responsible leaders — students who not only excel in their chosen fields but also contribute meaningfully to society and the world.

Our Mission

To deliver holistic, student-centered education through innovative pedagogy, experienced faculty, and world-class infrastructure — empowering every student to reach their full potential academically, physically, creatively, and morally.
